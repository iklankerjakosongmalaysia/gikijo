Learn More

Follow these easy steps to create a new post:

  • Click the "+ Create New Post" button.
  • Select the "+ Create Now" tab.
  • Fill in all the required information about the job post.
  • Click the "Create" button.
  • Your post is now ready!

Type: ViewUnlock

Follow these steps to publish your post:

  • Click the "Publish Now" button.
  • Congratulations! Your post is now published and ready to be viewed.
  • To see your published post, simply click the "View" button.
Type: DirectView

Follow these steps to publish your post:

  • Click the "Ready to publish? Activate now" button to activate your post.
  • Choose how many days you want your post to be active.
  • Ensure you have enough tokens for the publishing process. If not, recharge your tokens and try again.
  • If you have enough tokens, tick the confirmation checkbox and click the "Purchase" button.
  • Click the "Publish Now" button.
  • Congratulations! Your post is now published and ready to be viewed.
  • To see your published post, simply click the "View" button.

Follow these steps to share your post to our Telegram channels:

  • Click the "Share" button.
  • Select the channel where you want to share your post.
  • Click the "Share Now" button.
  • Click on paper plane icon next to the channel name to view your post on Telegram.

Warning! Editing a post won't update previous shares on our Telegram channel. Only the Gikijo website reflects changes. Delete the current share and repost to see updates.

Follow these steps to edit your post details:

  • Click the "Edit" button.
  • Make the desired changes to the details of your post.
  • Click the "Submit" button to save your edits.
  • To view your edited post, click the "View" button.

Follow these steps to view who has applied to your post:

  • Click on the "Applicants" button.
  • You will see a list of users who have applied for the job. If no one has applied yet, the list may be empty.
  • Click on the arrow button next to their name to expand their details.
  • To view the hidden information represented by asterisks (****) beside the star icon, click "Unlock Profile."
  • Click the confirmation checkbox and then click "Unlock Now" to proceed.
  • Once unlocked, you can view the previously hidden information.
  • To download the applicant's profile, click the "Download" button.

To view our Policy on Responsible Use and Violations, please click on the following link: Policy. This policy outlines guidelines for using our platform in a responsible and respectful manner, as well as the consequences for policy violations.

If you encounter any issues or have questions about this process, please don't hesitate to send us your feedback through the settings menu. We're committed to continuously improving our platform and appreciate your input.
