What is Job Slot

We offer a flexible job template called a Job Slot that you can edit and use to publish posts again at no extra cost while it's valid. Please be aware that once it expires, the Job Slot can no longer be used and any related posts published on our channels will be deleted.

You can update the details of your Job Slot even after it's been posted. However, please note that any posts that have already been published on our channels cannot be edited and can only be deleted. If you wish to publish a new post after deleting an existing one, simply delete the current published post and post a new one with the updated details of your Job Slot.

How to Create Job Slot and make first post

1. Create your new job slot

To begin, click the "Create a New Job Slot" button and select either the free or paid option. Fill out the required job information on the form, and then click "Create" to generate your job slot.

2. Activate your job slot

For paid job slots, click on the "Activate" button and complete the payment process to activate your job slot. Free job slots do not require activation.

3. Make your first post to our channels

Click the "Post" button on your job slot to share it to our channels of your choice. If needed, you can edit the job slot and make a new post, but you cannot modify an already published post. Instead, you can delete it and create a new post with the updated job slot.

Let us know if you're having issues

If you encounter any issues or have questions about this process, please don't hesitate to send us your feedback through the settings menu. We're committed to continuously improving our platform and appreciate your input.
