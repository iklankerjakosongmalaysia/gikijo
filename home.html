How it works?
1. Create a Job Slot

Choose between "Plus" or "Free" job slot. Fill in the details of your job opening in your job slot forms.

2. Activate your Job Slot

If you wish to use the Plus job slot, there are a few easy activation steps to follow. On the other hand, the free job slot doesn't require any activation.

3. Post your Job Slot

Post your job slot to our channels. Edit and post again if needed to keep the information up-to-date.
